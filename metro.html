Routes & lines

A Metro Route is the path or direction a metro train follows from a starting station to an ending station, stopping

Station list

A Station List is a complete, ordered list of all metro stations on a particular metro line or route, arranged from the starting station to the ending station.

Ticket fares

Ticket Fares are the amount of money passengers pay to travel on the metro from one station to another.
